About Fictr

Fictr organizes competitive esports events, supports players and teams, and creates opportunities for gamers across India.

Our Mission

We're committed to growing India's esports ecosystem by creating accessible tournaments, cultivating upcoming talent, and partnering with stakeholders to deliver high-quality competitive experiences.
Fictr promotes fair play and long-term player development.

Our Approach

Transparent, community-first formats. Admin support and moderation for every tournament, plus special opportunities for regional players.

Highlights

Showcase & media exposure for talented teams, fair play systems, and real growth for grassroots to flagship-level esports.
